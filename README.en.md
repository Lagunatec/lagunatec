# LagunaTec

**Transforming data into strategic intelligence.**

LagunaTec is a consultancy focused on **Data, Business Intelligence and Digital Transformation**.
We help organizations structure data, automate processes and turn information into strategic decisions.

Our mission is to empower companies to become **data-driven organizations**, combining modern technologies, analytics and business expertise.

We follow a structured methodology to ensure projects deliver measurable value.

### 2 — Architecture Design

Designing scalable data architectures aligned with business goals.
